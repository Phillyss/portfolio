De Interactieve Noedel Gadget

Project

This school team project was about solving a particul frustration you and your team have now everyone's mostly at home. We all had one frustration in common; dishes that are not in dishwashers and uncleaned kitchen equipment.

So we came up with a product to help counter this.

Product Video

Research

To design a solution to this problem we had to know how first. So we setup a research plan to answer the following questions:

What motivates people?
What possible ways are there to motivate each other to clean up?
How do you make chores fun to do?
How do you strengthen the feeling of satisfaction and pride of a person?

Ideation

With the answers we got from our research we went off to come up with many different concepts. Most of the concepts revovled around competition, rewards and music.

The idea we liked the most was D.I.N.G. a speaker friend to look after.

Concept

DING is a tamagotchi like bluetooth speaker in the appearance of a noodle monster. It's noodles shine light so it can also be used as a night light.

DING has it's own emotions, it will express happiness and play music. When it's fully content you'll be able to play music for 4 hours. The less time remaining the more it's mood goes down. When angry, DING will not play music anymore and express frustration.

To make DING happy again you need to charge up his happiness bar. You do so by cleaning up in the kitchen, where his docking station stands.
